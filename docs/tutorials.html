Tutorials¶

This section contains a list links to tutorials, which are all Jupyter Notebooks. It is highly recommended that rather than just reading through the material, you download the notebook files, run them on your own computer and play around with the code. They also contain short problems that will help you to learn various aspects of ISAMBARD.
